Did you know?

SM Supermalls, a network of shopping malls in the Philippines owned by SM Prime, has 76 malls around the nation as of early 2021, with 26 more set to open for a total of 102 malls. It also operates seven shopping malls in China, including SM Tianjin, the world's second-largest in terms of gross leasable area (GLA). SM Supermalls has grown to become one of Southeast Asia's largest mall operators. The total gross floor space of the corporation is about 9.24 million square meters (GFA). In the Philippines, it has 17,230 renters, while in China, it has 1,867 tenants.
